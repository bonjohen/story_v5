# Overcoming the Monster — Example Mapping

**Primary example:** Jaws (1975 film)
**Cross-references:** Jurassic Park (1993 film), Beowulf (Old English epic poem)

---

## Node Mappings

| Node ID | Node Label | Jaws Moment |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| OM_N01 | Peaceful State Under Threat | Amity Island is a summer beach town whose economy depends on tourism. The opening attack on Chrissie Watkins signals the threat, but the community suppresses it. |
| OM_N02 | Monster Revealed | The shark kills Alex Kintner in broad daylight at a crowded beach. The community can no longer deny the threat. Hooper's examination of the first victim confirms a great white. |
| OM_N03 | Call to Confront | Chief Brody, overruled by the mayor on closing the beaches, hires Quint after the bounty scene. Brody, Hooper, and Quint form the hunting party. Brody commits despite his fear of water. |
| OM_N04 | Preparation and Reconnaissance | Hooper brings scientific equipment — a shark cage, tracking devices, analysis tools. Quint contributes the Orca, harpoons, and barrels. Hooper's necropsy of the tiger shark proves it's not the right one. The real shark's size is estimated. |
| OM_N05 | Initial Encounter | The first day at sea. The shark reveals itself — far larger than expected. Barrels are attached but the shark dives and drags them under. The team realizes their equipment is inadequate against this animal. |
| OM_N06 | Nightmare Phase | The shark attacks the Orca directly, flooding the engine room. Quint smashes the radio to prevent calling for help. The USS Indianapolis monologue reveals Quint's psychological damage. The boat is sinking. Hooper's cage dive fails — the shark destroys the cage. Quint is killed. |
| OM_N07 | Final Confrontation | Brody, alone on the sinking stern, shoves a pressurized scuba tank into the shark's mouth. As the shark approaches for the kill, Brody shoots the tank — the shark explodes. Victory requires improvisation, nerve, and accepting he's the last one standing. |
| OM_N08 | World Restored | Brody and Hooper swim to shore on the remains of the barrels. Amity Island is safe. But Quint is dead, the Orca is destroyed, and Brody will never be the same man who arrived from New York. |

**Jurassic Park cross-reference:** Peaceful state is the island before the park opens to visitors; the monster is revealed when the T. rex escapes during the storm; the call to confront is Grant taking responsibility for the children; preparation is Muldoon and Ellie restoring the park systems; the nightmare phase is the raptor pursuit through the compound; the final confrontation is the kitchen and visitor center sequence; the world is restored when the survivors escape by helicopter.

**Beowulf cross-reference:** Peaceful state is Heorot hall under Grendel's shadow; the monster is revealed through Grendel's nightly attacks; the call to confront is Beowulf's arrival from Geatland; preparation is Beowulf's decision to fight without weapons; the initial encounter is the fight with Grendel; the nightmare is Grendel's mother's retaliatory attack; the final confrontation is the underwater battle with Grendel's mother (and later, the dragon); the world is restored but at the cost of Beowulf's life.

## Edge Mappings

| Edge ID | Edge Label | Jaws Moment |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| OM_E01 | Threat Strikes | Chrissie Watkins is killed in the opening scene. The disruption is immediate — but the mayor's coverup delays the community's reckoning. |
| OM_E02 | Accepts the Mission | Brody commits after the Kintner boy's death and the mayor's refusal to close the beaches. The bounty meeting produces Quint's offer. Brody accepts despite his hydrophobia. |
| OM_E03 | Gathers Tools and Knowledge | Hooper's forensic analysis and equipment, Quint's seamanship and harpoons. The preparation is split between scientific and practical expertise. |
| OM_E04 | First Engagement | "You're gonna need a bigger boat." The shark surfaces and the team grasps the true scale of what they face. Previous assumptions are shattered. |
| OM_E05 | Overwhelmed | The shark systematically dismantles the Orca. Quint's desperation leads him to burn out the engine. The hunters become the hunted. Each countermeasure fails. |
| OM_E06 | Key Insight Gained | Brody's improvised plan — the scuba tank as a bomb — emerges from desperation, not preparation. The insight is that brute force won't work; the shark must be destroyed from within. |
| OM_E07 | Monster Falls | The shark explodes. The ocean goes quiet. The cost is visible: Quint dead, the boat gone, Brody and Hooper barely alive. Amity is saved but the victory is not clean. |
